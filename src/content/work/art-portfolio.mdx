### Julie Moss Fine Art

This website, built using React & Gatsby, provides Julie Moss with a custom-built site tailored to her needs. Its clean, minimalist design effectively highlights her vibrant artwork while offering an intuitive user experience.

The site's performance is exceptional, achieving near-perfect Lighthouse scores across
all metrics; performance, accessibility, best practices, and SEO. The static site
generation of Gatsby, combined with Netlify's continuous deployment and hosting,
ensures fast load times and seamless updates, making it ideal for presenting her
portfolio online.

A custom built CMS was delivered to give Julie a simple easy way to upload her art and blog articles.
